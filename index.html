Bringing change to Lexington

"It is my privilege to dedicate my entire adult life to serve as one of the top chefs in the world, TV, and hopefully as your constable. While my service has been done all around the world, I am excited to narrow in on Lexington. During my years of experience, I established a reputation as a committed, competent and strong leader who works to address our most difficult issues by building smart, diverse teams. Most importantly, I listen carefully to the concerns of the people I represent. I believe a government of the people works best when it listens to its people. I want to be your constable to lead Lexington to an ever-brighter future: the home where families are safe and welcome; the place where plentiful jobs offer purpose, security and pride; the destination city it can and should be; and where vibrant community provides a rich array of opportunity. "
